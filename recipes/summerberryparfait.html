Summer Berry Parfait

Description

The search for a simple, tasty, and nutritious dessert is finally over. Cure your sweet tooth with this summer berry parfait that is perfect for the summer months. This parfait can be made for a delicious breakfast or a midday snack. Enjoy it using fresh or frozen blueberries or fresh strawberries.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup sliced strawberries
  • 3/4 cup blueberries
  • 6 ounces of vanilla yogurt (preferably greek)
  • 1 tablespoon wheat germ
  • 1/2 banana, sliced
  • 1/3 cup granola

Steps

  1. Layer 1/4 cup strawberries, 1/4 cup blueberries, 1/3 container yogurt, 1/3 tablespoon wheat germ, 1/3 of the sliced banana, and about 2 tablespoons of granola in a large bowl. Continue to the the parfait, repeating the layers until all of the ingredients are used.
